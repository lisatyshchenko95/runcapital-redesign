Our Asset Management practice is delivered by Run Asset Management, our Luxembourg-domiciled entity. We work with family offices, institutional investors, and private wealth clients to structure and operate the vehicles that hold their alternative investments.

Every mandate is independent — we have no affiliation with any banking, financial, or insurance group. Our only obligation is to our clients, and our fee structure is aligned with that principle.

How It Works

A clear path from mandate to market.

Every engagement follows a transparent four-stage process. You always know where we are and what comes next.

01

Strategy & Structure

We work with you to understand the investment thesis, investor profile, and regulatory constraints. We recommend the right vehicle — AIF, securitization SPV, note program, or hybrid structure.

02

Formation & Documentation

We handle the full Luxembourg setup: legal documentation, CSSF filings, service provider onboarding (depositary, administrator, auditor), and issuance prospectus.

03

Launch & Operations

We coordinate the capital raise, subscription processing, and ongoing operational workflow. NAV calculation, investor reporting, and regulatory filings happen on schedule, every cycle.

04

Ongoing Stewardship

We remain your partner for the life of the vehicle: performance reporting, investor relations, regulatory change management, and strategic reviews when the mandate evolves.

Securitization Vehicles

  • Tax-efficient structuring

Structured notes, private placements, and bond programs — issued from our Luxembourg platform with ISIN allocation and clearing readiness.

  • Vehicle migration & relocation
