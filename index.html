About Us

Independent. Unaffiliated.
Built for your interests.

Since 2018, Run Capital Partners has supported enterprises in strategic financial management. We accompany clients through governance, legal and financial structuring of alternative investment funds, and the creation of securitization vehicles.


We operate independently, without affiliation to any banking, financial, or insurance group. Our only obligation is to our clients. Our team brings over 30 years of combined experience from institutions including JP Morgan, Pictet, and Banca Commerciale Italiana.
