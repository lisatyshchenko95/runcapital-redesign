How It Works

From private company to public market.

A staged roadmap that prepares the company, executes the listing, and supports the life as a public company.

01

Readiness Assessment

We assess the company against Nasdaq listing standards: financials, governance, reporting capability, market positioning. We identify gaps and set a realistic timeline.

02

Preparation

Audit readiness, corporate reorganization, governance upgrades, equity story refinement, and investor targeting. We introduce underwriters, US counsel, auditors, and IR partners.

03

Listing Execution

Prospectus, SEC filings, roadshow, pricing, and listing day. We stay alongside the management team through the entire sequence, coordinating the full advisor stack.

04

Post-Listing Advisory

Life as a public company is a different discipline. We advise on quarterly reporting cadence, investor relations, secondary offerings, and the ongoing regulatory rhythm.
