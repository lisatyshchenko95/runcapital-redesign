Service 03 Cross-asset structuring

Structured Products.

Design and distribution across all asset classes. Private placements, listed securities, and Pre-IPO access.

— What it is

A security with a defined payoff.

A structured product is a single security — packaged by a bank, linked to an underlying (equity, index, rate, commodity or credit), with a payoff and a maturity agreed up front.

— In practice

Defined Payoff
Coupons, barriers, participation — all specified at issue.
Defined Maturity
A set term, from a few months to several years.
One ISIN
Held in standard custody, subscribed like a bond.

— How We Distribute

Two routes to market.

We work with a wide network of primary issuers to build structured products across equities, fixed income, FX, commodities and credit — and distribute them two ways.
