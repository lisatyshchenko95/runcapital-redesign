RCP Global Markets

The UK advisory and distribution arm of Run Capital Partners. London-based, FCA Appointed Representative, serving institutional and private clients across advisory, sport & wealth, and structured product distribution.

Speak with London Our Capabilities

Overview

London-based advisory and distribution.

Run Capital Partners Global Markets Ltd operates from London as the group's UK advisory and distribution arm, connecting Southern European capital with institutional-grade opportunities across the City and beyond.

As an FCA Appointed Representative, the London team provides regulated advisory services, distributes the group's structured products and alternative investment solutions, and leads client coverage in the sport & wealth vertical. The entity works in close coordination with the group's Luxembourg and Italian platforms to deliver end-to-end execution.

Capabilities

What the London entity delivers.

Four focused capabilities at the intersection of UK regulation, European origination, and global distribution.

Advisory

Regulated investment advisory to institutional clients, family offices, and sophisticated private investors. Portfolio construction, manager selection, and bespoke mandates delivered through FCA-authorised channels.

Distribution

Distribution of the group's structured products, certificates, and alternative investment solutions across the UK and broader European investor base. Institutional placement and intermediary coverage.

Sport & Wealth

Dedicated wealth advisory for professional athletes, agents, and sporting institutions. Career-aware portfolio construction, currency management, and long-term financial planning tailored to the realities of a sporting career.

Structured Products & Alternatives Partner

The group's UK counterparty for structured product origination and alternative strategies. Bridges UK distribution with Luxembourg issuance and Italian advisory to deliver cohesive cross-border execution.
