5. Risk Warnings

Investment involves risk, including the risk of loss of capital. Alternative investment funds, securitization vehicles, structured products, and algorithmically managed portfolios carry specific risks that investors should understand before investing. These include, without limitation:

  • Market risk: values can fall as well as rise due to market movements.
  • Liquidity risk: some vehicles have limited or no secondary market.
  • Counterparty risk: including in structured products issued by investment banks.
  • Leverage risk: where leverage is used, losses can exceed the initial investment.
  • Regulatory and tax risk: changes in law or tax treatment may affect outcomes.
  • Currency risk: where the investment is in a currency other than your base currency.

Past performance is not indicative of future results. Before investing, read the full offering documents for the relevant vehicle and take independent advice where appropriate.

6. Conflicts of Interest

Run Capital Partners operates independently of banking, insurance, and financial groups. We nonetheless identify, manage, and disclose conflicts of interest in line with applicable regulation. Our conflicts of interest policy is available on request from info@runcapital.partners.

7. Complaints

If you have a complaint about any service provided by a group entity, please contact info@runcapital.partners or the entity directly. Where a complaint cannot be resolved to your satisfaction, you may have the right to refer it to the relevant supervisory authority, including:

  • UK: Financial Ombudsman Service (for eligible complainants).
  • Luxembourg: CSSF out-of-court complaint resolution procedure.
  • Italy: ACF, IVASS, or OAM depending on the service.
