Capabilities

What Run Asset Management does.

Securitization

Structuring and administration under the Luxembourg Securitisation Law. Bespoke instruments that turn illiquid exposures into tradable form for institutional investors.

Alternative Investment Funds (AIF)

Authorised AIF management across private equity, private credit, and multi-strategy mandates. Full AIFM services: risk, portfolio management, valuation, and reporting.

Securitization Vehicles (SPV)

Setup and administration of Luxembourg SPVs for single-asset, single-strategy, or compartmentalised exposures. Ring-fenced structures for club deals and co-investments.

Holding Arrangements

Holding structures for family offices, entrepreneurs, and institutional groups. Tax-aware, treaty-efficient arrangements for businesses and financial assets.
