Run Asset Management S.à.r.l

The Luxembourg-based Alternative Investment Fund Manager of the Run Capital Partners Group. Regulated by the CSSF, Run Asset Management is the institutional backbone of our fund infrastructure, managing vehicles that serve private clients, family offices, and institutions across Europe.

Overview

Luxembourg AIFM,
at the heart of European fund infrastructure.

Run Asset Management S.à.r.l operates from Luxembourg — Europe's foremost fund jurisdiction — providing the regulated management layer for the group's alternative investment strategies.

Authorised and supervised by the Commission de Surveillance du Secteur Financier (CSSF), Run Asset Management structures and manages alternative investment funds, securitisation vehicles, and holding arrangements for the Run Capital Partners Group.

The entity combines the regulatory rigour of Luxembourg with the investment philosophy of the wider group: patient capital, privileged access, and long-term alignment with clients. Every vehicle is built to institutional standards and administered with the discipline that a CSSF-regulated manager requires.

Capabilities

What Run Asset Management does.

Four core capabilities delivered within a CSSF-regulated Luxembourg framework.

Securitization

Structuring and administration of securitisation transactions under the Luxembourg Securitisation Law. Bespoke instruments that transform illiquid exposures into tradable, investable form for institutional and professional investors.

Alternative Investment Funds (AIF)

Authorised management of alternative investment funds across private equity, private credit, and multi-strategy mandates. Full AIFM services including risk management, portfolio management, valuation, and regulatory reporting.

Securitization Vehicles (SPV)

Establishment and ongoing administration of Luxembourg SPVs for single-asset, single-strategy, or compartmentalised exposures. Efficient, ring-fenced structures for club deals, co-investments, and dedicated mandates.

Holding Arrangements

Design and management of holding structures for family offices, entrepreneurs, and institutional groups. Tax-aware, treaty-efficient arrangements for long-term ownership of operating businesses, financial assets, and real estate.
