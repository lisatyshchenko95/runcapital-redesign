What We Do

Italian capabilities.

Corporate advisory, regulated insurance brokerage, and cross-border coordination with the Group.

01 · Corporate Consultancy

Strategic, financial, and operational advisory for corporate and institutional clients. Private client and individual wealth mandates are handled by other Group entities under their own authorisations.

02 · Insurance Brokerage

Regulated under IVASS Section E (Reg. No. E000657518). The team structures and places insurance solutions for corporate and institutional clients, with access to Italian and international carriers.

03 · Cross-border Coordination

Italy works with the UK and Luxembourg entities to deliver joined-up advisory. Clients with multi-market exposures are served by a single Group relationship.
