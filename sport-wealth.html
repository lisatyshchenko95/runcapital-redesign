Sport & Wealth

Bespoke wealth management and alternative-investment structures for professional athletes, sports-related high-net-worth clients, and institutional mandates with a sports footprint.

Contact Us About This Service How It Works

Overview

A career-cycle approach
to wealth in sport.

Professional athletes and sports executives face a wealth challenge few advisors understand: high earnings concentrated in a short career window, unusual contract structures, image rights, and the complexity of multi-jurisdictional residency. Off-the-shelf wealth management falls short.

Our Sport & Wealth practice, led by Massimo Bocci, designs financial strategies for the full career arc — from first major contract through retirement transition. We also partner with institutional investors whose mandates touch the sport economy, structuring vehicles that serve both sides of the market.

How It Works

Discretion, then design.

A confidential process built around the realities of professional sport and institutional sports mandates.

01

Discovery

Confidential review of the client’s career stage, earning profile, contract structure, residency, family situation, and long-term objectives. No two mandates begin the same way.

02

Structuring

We design the right vehicle set: wealth holding structures, alternative-investment allocations, image-rights management, and cross-border tax alignment. Every structure is built for discretion and durability.

03

Implementation

We coordinate the rollout across legal, banking, and investment providers. You work with one point of contact — Massimo — and one team that knows the full picture.

04

Long-Term Stewardship

Careers evolve. Contracts end, families grow, residency changes. We remain your partner through each transition, adjusting the strategy as life changes, not after.
