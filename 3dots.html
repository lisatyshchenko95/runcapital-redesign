About 3DOTS

European companies, US public markets.

Southern European companies often have the strength, the customer base, and the growth profile to thrive on the public markets — but the path to a US listing is unfamiliar territory.

3DOTS was founded inside Run Capital Partners as a dedicated practice for this transition. Today it operates as an independent spinoff with the same conviction: Europe builds great companies, and the US capital markets are the right venue for many of them. We make the journey navigable.

How It Works

From private company
to public market.

A staged roadmap that prepares the company, executes the listing, and supports life as a public issuer.

01 · Readiness Assessment

We evaluate the company against Nasdaq listing standards: financials, governance, reporting capability, market positioning. We identify gaps and set a realistic timeline.

02 · Preparation

Audit readiness, corporate reorganization, governance upgrades, equity story refinement, and investor targeting. We introduce underwriters, US counsel, auditors, and IR partners.

03 · Listing Execution

Prospectus, SEC filings, roadshow, pricing, and listing day. We stay alongside the management team through the entire sequence, coordinating the full advisor stack.

04 · Post-Listing Advisory

Quarterly reporting cadence, investor relations, secondary offerings, and the ongoing regulatory rhythm of a US-listed company.

Relationship to Run Capital Partners
